Welcome to my personal blog! This is where I'll be sharing my thoughts on coding, technology, and everything I learn along the way.

## Why I started this blog

I've always believed that the best way to learn is to **teach**. Writing forces you to organize your thoughts and truly understand a topic. This blog is my way of:

1. Documenting my learning journey
2. Sharing useful tips and tricks
3. Building in public

## What to expect

You can look forward to posts about:

- **Web Development** — Frontend, backend, and everything in between
- **DevOps** — CI/CD, deployment, infrastructure
- **Tools & Productivity** — Editor setups, CLI tools, workflows
- **Project Walkthroughs** — Step-by-step guides
