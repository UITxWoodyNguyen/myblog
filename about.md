## 🚩 CTF & Security

I compete in **Capture The Flag** (CTF) competitions as part of **UIT.k0dunqtool**, focusing primarily on:

- 🔍 **Reverse Engineering** — static/dynamic analysis, binary patching
- 🔐 **Cryptography** — classical ciphers to modern crypto attacks
- 🌐 **Web Exploitation** — OWASP Top 10, injection, auth bypasses
- 🐚 **Pwn / Binary Exploitation** — buffer overflows, ROP chains
